We want you to get the most out of DataMagic and are here to help. If you have any questions or need assistance:

 On the top right, click the Feedback button

 Feedback is responded to as soon as possible during UK business hours

Bug Reports

Despite our best efforts, bugs do occasionally slip through the net. If you experience an issue with DataMagic, please follow the steps below to report it to our support team:

 Navigate to the page were you are experiencing the issue

 Click the Feedback button

 Ensure the Type shown is Bug Report

 Enter a Summary and in the Description box, provide as much detail as possible about the issue you are experiencing, including:
 
 What you were doing when the issue occurred

 What you expected to happen

 What actually happened

 Any error messages you received

 
 

 Click Submit
 
 A message showing your ticket number appears - you may wish to note this for future reference

 What actually happened

 Any error messages you received

 

One of our team will be in touch and may ask for further information and screenshots.

Feature Requests

We welcome ideas for new features and improvements and treat customer-requested features as priority, often including them in the next release. To submit a request for a new feature:

 Click the Feedback button

 From the Type dropdown, select Feature Request

 Enter a description of the requested feature, with much detail as you can provide then click Submit

A message showing your ticket number appears - you may wish to note this for future reference.

Support Requests

Some features, such as remote SQL access, require additional configuration and setup. If you require assistance with these or anything else:

 Click the Feedback button

 From the Type dropdown, select Support Request

 Enter a Summary and in the Description box, provide details of the support we can provide

 Click Submit

A message showing your ticket number appears - you may wish to note this for future reference.
