General

 
 What is NCalc101?

 NCalc101 is a web-based calculator for evaluating mathematical expressions, supporting variables, functions, and workspaces.
 

 
 Do I need to create an account?

 No account is required for basic use. Some features, like saving workspaces, may require you to sign in.
 

Expressions

 
 What operators are supported?

 NCalc101 supports +, -, *, /, ^, and parentheses for grouping.
 

 
 Can I use functions?

 Yes! Use functions like sqrt(), sin(), log(), and more.
 

Variables

 
 How do I define a variable?

 Assign a value using =, e.g., x = 10.
 

 
 Are variable names case-sensitive?

 Yes, total and Total are different variables.
 

 
 How do I clear or reset a variable?

 Assign a new value or use the workspace management features to reset.
 

Workspaces

 
 What is a workspace?

 A workspace is a collection of your expressions, variables, and results, organized for a specific project or topic.
 

 
 Can I have multiple workspaces?

 Yes, you can create and switch between multiple workspaces.
 

Errors and Troubleshooting

 
 Why do I see an error message?

 Errors usually mean there’s a typo, an undefined variable, or an unsupported operation in your expression.
 

 
 How do I fix calculation errors?

 Double-check your syntax, variable names, and function usage. Refer to the Expressions and Variables help pages for guidance.

 Where can I find more examples?

 Visit the Examples page for ready-to-use sample expressions.
