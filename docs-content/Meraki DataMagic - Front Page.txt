Use DataMagic to manage, monitor and maintain the data in your Cisco Meraki networks. With a user-friendly interface and a dedicated, historical datamart, you can:

 Get a long-term, comprehensive view across multiple organizations

 Easily filter data, (find out which Meraki administrators do not have 2FA), create and export reports, and visualize network trends over time

 Use your own SQL queries to analyze historical data, correlate network events

 Troubleshoot issues by examining a device's entire configuration history. For example, identify a DHCP conflict and see exactly when a configuration change was made that may have caused it.

 See a detailed timeline of configuration changes, crucial for auditing and compliance, and a full historical record of all network events, wireless connection failures, and other key metrics

 Track device lifecycles, monitor for security threats, and ensure consistency in network configurations

 Export data as XLSX to streamline reporting and share with other teams or clients
